About NextBus

Our Mission

NextBus is a smart college bus tracking system designed to make student commutes more reliable and efficient. Our platform provides real-time bus tracking, route information, and schedule management to help students and faculty plan their journeys with confidence.

Features

  • Real-time bus location tracking
  • Detailed route information and stops
  • Estimated arrival times
  • Mobile-friendly interface
  • Popular route suggestions
